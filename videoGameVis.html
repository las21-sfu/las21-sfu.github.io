Reflection

The visualization highlights clear dominance patterns across platforms and genres. Certain platforms generate significantly higher global sales, particularly when paired with popular genres like Action and Sports. The darker and larger circles indicate strong market concentration, showing that a limited number of platform–genre combinations account for a substantial share of total sales. Strategy titles vary depending on platform strength, reinforcing how platform popularity strongly influences genre performance.
