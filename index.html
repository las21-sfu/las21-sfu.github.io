My Work

Aquarium Life

Aquarium Life is an interactive aquarium simulation where users care for a digital tank by adjusting water settings, feeding fish through drag-and-drop interactions, cleaning algae, and adding new fish. The project focuses on exploratory interaction to create an immersive and relaxing experience.

Kinetic Typography Video

This project explores motion-based typography and graphics to communicate the emotion of a selected audio clip. It applies key animation and motion-design principles to create a visually engaging short video.

Issue Reporting Platform for SIAT Students

This team project focused on designing an issue reporting platform accessible on both web and mobile for SIAT students. Our goal was to promote transparent, community-driven problem-solving and keep faculty informed of recurring issues.
